Government schemes are vital in supporting farmers by offering financial aid, resources, and expertise to enhance agricultural practices and ensure sustainability. Through subsidies, loans, and skill development programs, farmers are empowered to improve their livelihoods and contribute to the growth of the agricultural sector.
